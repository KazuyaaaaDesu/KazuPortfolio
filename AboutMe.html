About Me


Not good with words, so i paint instead.
It's easier to explain a feeling when I can shape it with color and light.
My artworks is like that one dream you forgot but still feel.
Or a song that ruins your day in a good way.
And maybe its also about ghosts of people's leftover feelings,
Or words that were never said.
If you've ever looked at a painting and seen in a way that hurt a little,
that's what I hope to create.
